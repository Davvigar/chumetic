Our History

2010

The company was founded with a focus on innovation, aiming to revolutionize the automotive industry with cutting-edge technology and unparalleled design.

2015

International expansion began, bringing our products to new markets across Europe and North America, establishing a strong global presence.

2020

A decade of success and new horizons. We celebrated our 10th anniversary with the launch of a new line of eco-friendly vehicles, setting new standards in sustainability.

2025

Continuing our legacy of innovation, we introduced advanced AI-driven customization options, allowing customers to personalize their vehicles like never before. Our commitment to excellence remains unwavering as we look towards the future.
